Favorite Keyboards and Switches

Here are some of my favorite keyboards and switches that provide a great typing feel and sound:

  • Aula F75: A compact 75% layout with a gasket mount design that delivers a smooth, deep sound.
  • Lingbao K75: Another excellent choice in its segment, featuring a solid and sturdy build.
  • Leobog Graywood: A linear switch with a signature thocky sound and a smooth, scratch-free keystroke.
